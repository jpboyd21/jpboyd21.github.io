Lessons from Building with AI

What Manus, ChatGPT, and Notion have taught me

Posted by Jacob Boyd on April 24, 2025

This semester, I dove headfirst into building real-world tools with AI. My favorite project? A recruiter bot that I use to automatically respond to leads on Instagram. I used Manus AI to power the backend logic, and honestly — it's changed the game.

I’ve learned that the best tools don’t just work — they work for you. Building with AI is about iterating fast, testing things you don’t fully understand yet, and constantly refining. It’s a craft, not a cheat code.

Whether it’s a scratch page, a resume site, or an app that makes people smile, the same rule applies: create with purpose. Don’t just ship something that works — ship something that matters.
