About Me

AI Enthusiast • Builder • Writer

Hey, I'm Jacob Boyd — a student at BYU studying Management Information Systems. I work at the intersection of technology, creativity, and grit. I build tools, write stories, and explore ways to make life more efficient, meaningful, and connected.

Some of my current projects include a job-hunting AI toolkit for students, a tutoring startup called GoStudy, and a digital journaling series called Urban Pilgrim. I’m also the Outreach President for the BYU AI in Business Society and will be doing summer sales with V3 Electric.

Outside of all that? I’m in the gym six days a week trying to bulk up, always down for deep conversations, and quietly preparing for whatever life throws next.
